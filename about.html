About Us: We're warm, friendly and creative

Reflections is the dream and passion of Yvonne Tillock. A retired businesswoman, Yvonne personally hand-makes the products; “a labour of love” is how she describes the creative and manufacturing process.

That love can be seen in the care and attention to detail of the Reflections products. They combine the vibrant colours of the local flora & fauna with simple functionality and timeless style.
